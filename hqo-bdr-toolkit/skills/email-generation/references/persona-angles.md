# Persona Angles & Email Templates

## Universal Email Template

```
Hi [Name],

[Personalized opener — tied to news/promotion/portfolio detail relevant to their role]

[Pain hypothesis from persona table below, adapted to their specific portfolio/market]

At [Customer Parallel], the [peer group] uses [HqO value from table] — [specific outcome: visibility, efficiency, retention, etc.].

Worth a [quick call / 15-minute conversation] to see how other [peer group] are approaching this?

Best,
[Sender]
```

## Persona Reference Table

| Persona | Pain Hypothesis | HqO Value Hook | Peer Group |
|---------|----------------|---------------|------------|
| Asset Manager | No system to measure tenant health; churn risk surfaces at renewal, not before; no visibility into biggest NOI lever: tenant LTV | Dashboards connecting experience metrics to retention and NOI; asset health scores; investor-grade insight | owner-operators / institutional owners |
| Property Manager | Managing tenant requests across email, phone, walk-ups with no single system for response times, satisfaction, or recurring issues | Single platform to track requests, automate follow-ups, surface recurring issues before escalation | property teams |
| Experience Manager | Running events/programming but no way to measure what drives engagement or which activities tenants value most | Platform to promote events, track attendance, collect feedback — know exactly what's working | experience managers |
| Regional Director | Overseeing multiple properties but no standardized view of tenant satisfaction or ops performance across the region | Portfolio-wide dashboard for engagement, satisfaction, and operational metrics across all properties in real time | regional directors |
| Marketing/Comms | Sending tenant communications with no visibility into open rates, engagement, or which messages land | Centralized comms platform to push announcements, track engagement, see what's being read | CRE marketing teams |
| IT/Digital | Patchwork of disconnected building systems with no unified data layer; IT seen as cost center | Centralized platform integrating existing systems into single source of truth; secure APIs | CRE IT leaders |
| Building Engineer | Tracking equipment issues and maintenance via spreadsheets and paper logs; no mobile-first tools | Mobile-first platform for logging issues, tracking maintenance schedules, surfacing recurring problems | engineering teams |

## Subject Line Formulas

**Trigger-based:** "Congrats on [news] — quick thought on [angle]" · "[Company]'s [news] — [angle] angle" · "Saw [news] — relevant for [topic]"

**Social proof:** "What [Customer] learned about [topic]" · "[Customer] suggested we connect" · "How [Customer] approaches [topic]"

**Pain-based:** "Tenant [retention/visibility/engagement] at [Company]" · "[Topic] at [Company]'s [Market] portfolio" · "Seeing [X]% [metric]? Here's what [Company] did"

**Role-based:** "Quick thought for [Company]'s [role]" · "[Topic] for [Region/Portfolio]"
